Cymbal Travel Cancellation Policy

1. 24-Hour Free Cancellation: All bookings can be cancelled within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund.
2. Flights: After 24 hours, airline rules apply. Non-refundable tickets may receive travel credit.
3. Hotels: Cancellations made less than 48 hours before check-in may incur a one-night charge.
4. Cars: Prepaid rentals are refundable up to 48 hours before pick-up.
5. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
